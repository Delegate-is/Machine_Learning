KEY BUSINESS RULES (DO NOT BREAK)
------------------------------------
1. Dairy is primary revenue engine.
2. True cost per litre must always be calculable.
3. All income and expense must go through Transaction model.
4. Inventory must reflect real stock levels.
5. System must remain scalable to PostgreSQL.

------------------------------------

ANALYTICS LOGIC REFERENCE
------------------------------------
Cost per Litre = Total Dairy Costs / Total Milk Produced
Feed Efficiency = Litres Produced / Kg Feed Used
Crop ROI = (Revenue - Total Cost) / Total Cost
Net Profit = Total Income - Total Expense
